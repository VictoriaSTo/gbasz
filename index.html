Global Talent Registration Centre

We help you to build your professional and business future in mainland China

Our Global Registration Centre (GRC) provides a timely and responsive service to help you access our resources to advance your skills, ideas and business in innovation and digital technology. Fintechport, which is part of the GRC, is designed to help high-level talent and startups enter the Chinese job market or receive support to launch or expand their innovative business in China.

The GRC also aims to inspire and nurture the next generation in tech development and innovation by organizing GRAD (Games, Robots, Art, and Drones) Summer Camps and eSports tournaments.
